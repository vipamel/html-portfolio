The Best Movies According to Nolan

My top 3 Movies of all-time.

Pitch Black (film)

Pitch Black is the greatest combination of science fiction, horror and drama all in one movie. This movie was the beginnings of The chronicals of Riddick.

Prometheus (2012 film)

Prometheus was about seeking the origins of humanity, the crew arrives on a distant world and discovers a threat that could cause the extinction of the human species.

Deep Cover (1992 Film)

A uniformed police officer is recruited by the DEA to infiltrate a drug smuggling ring looking to expand its operation. The film stars Laurence Fishburne, a great suspence action movie.
